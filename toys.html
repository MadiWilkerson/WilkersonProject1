what kind of toys are good for my dog?

Dog toys are important for keeping dogs mentally stimulated, physically active, and entertained. They come in many types, including chew toys for teething and dental health, plush toys for comfort and play, and interactive toys like puzzle feeders that challenge a dog's mind. Toys also help reduce boredom and destructive behavior by giving dogs a safe outlet for their energy. Choosing durable, size-appropriate toys that match a dog's age, breed, and chewing style helps ensure both safety and enjoyment.
