what is a german sheppard?

The German Shepherd is a large, intelligent working dog breed known for its loyalty, strength, and trainability. Originally bred in Germany for herding and guarding, they are now commonly used in police, military, and service work due to their obedience and keen awareness. German Shepherds have thick double coats, usually in black and tan or sable colors, and are very active dogs that need regular exercise and mental stimulation. They are protective and devoted to their families, making them excellent companions when properly trained and socialized.
