The Basenji is a small, elegant dog breed originally from Central Africa, known for its intelligence, independence, and unique vocalization that sounds more like a yodel than a bark. Bred as a hunting dog, the Basenji has a strong prey drive and a keen sense of sight and smell. They have short, fine coats, curled tails, and a sleek, athletic build. Often described as cat-like in their grooming habits, Basenjis are very clean and low-odor dogs. While affectionate with their families, they can be stubborn and require consistent training, regular exercise, and mental stimulation to stay well-behaved and happy.
