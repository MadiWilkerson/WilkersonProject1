what is a shiba inu?

The Shiba Inu is a small to medium-sized dog breed from Japan known for its fox-like appearance, curled tail, and bold, independent personality. Originally bred for hunting, Shiba Inus are agile, alert, and very intelligent. They have a thick double coat that sheds seasonally and comes in colors such as red, black and tan, and sesame. While they are loyal and affectionate with their families, they can be stubborn and require consistent training, early socialization, and regular exercise to stay well-behaved and happy.
