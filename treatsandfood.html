what kind of treats would my dog like?

Treats and food are essential parts of a dog's diet and overall well-being. High-quality dog food provides the nutrients dogs need for energy, growth, and health, while treats are best used in moderation for training and rewards. A balanced diet should include the right mix of protein, fats, vitamins, and minerals based on a dog's age, size, and activity level. Fresh water should always be available, and portion control is important to prevent overeating and maintain a healthy weight.
