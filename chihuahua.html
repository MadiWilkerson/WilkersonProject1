The Chihuahua is a tiny dog breed known for its big personality, alert nature, and strong bond with its owner. Originating in Mexico, it is one of the smallest dog breeds, typically weighing under six pounds. Chihuahuas can have either short or long coats and come in many colors and patterns. Despite their small size, they are confident, energetic, and often very loyal, making them great companion dogs. They do well in smaller living spaces but still need regular attention, gentle training, and socialization to prevent nervous or overly protective behavior.
