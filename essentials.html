Dog essentials are the basic items every dog needs to stay healthy, safe, and happy. These include high-quality food and fresh water, sturdy food and water bowls, a comfortable bed, and a properly fitted collar with an ID tag. A leash and harness are important for walks and safety, while toys and chew items help with mental stimulation and preventing boredom. Grooming supplies like a brush, nail clippers, and shampoo are also essential for hygiene, along with regular vet care and vaccinations to support overall health.
