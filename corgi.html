The Corgi is a small herding dog known for its short legs, long body, and lively personality. Originally bred in Wales to herd cattle, Corgis are intelligent, energetic, and highly trainable. They have thick double coats that shed regularly and come in colors like red, sable, and tricolor. Friendly and affectionate, Corgis are great companions but need daily exercise and mental stimulation to stay happy and prevent boredom.
